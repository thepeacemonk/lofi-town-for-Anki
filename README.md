# Lofi.Town for Anki

Focus on your studies with **Lofi Town**, now integrated directly into Anki!

## Features

- **Lofi Focus Window**: Access the full Lofi.Town experience without leaving Anki. Listen to curated lofi beats and use focus tools to boost your productivity.
- **Deck Browser Widget**: A cute companion widget on your deck browser.
  - **Quick Access**: Open the Lofi Town window with a single click.
  - **Smart Controls**: Hover over the widget to access settings or refresh the add-on.
- **Seamless Integration**:
  - **Always on Top**: Option to keep the Lofi Town window visible while you study.
  - **Customizable**: Hide the deck widget if you prefer a cleaner look.
  - **Theme Aware**: The interface adapts to Anki's light or dark mode.

## Usage

### Opening Lofi Town
You can open the main window in three ways:
1. Click the **Open lofi.town** button on the deck browser widget.
2. Go to **Tools** ‚Üí **lofi.town** ‚Üí **Open lofi.town** in the menu bar.
3. Use the shortcut: `Shift + L`.

### Settings
Configure the add-on to suit your workflow:
- Go to **Tools** ‚Üí **lofi.town** ‚Üí **Settings**.
- Or click the **Gear icon** on the deck widget.

**Options:**
- **Always on Top**: Keeps the music window above other windows.
- **Hide Widget**: Removes the bunny widget from your deck browser.
